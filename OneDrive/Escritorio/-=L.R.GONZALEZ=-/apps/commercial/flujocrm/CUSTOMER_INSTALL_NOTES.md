## Local Data

FlujoCRM stores its app database locally at:

`%APPDATA%\FlujoCRM\data\flujocrm.db`

The app is designed to work without a cloud account. The pilot build does not
send your CRM database to MEDIOEVO by default.

Uninstalling the app removes the installed program and shortcuts. Local user
data may remain so you do not lose your CRM database by accident.
