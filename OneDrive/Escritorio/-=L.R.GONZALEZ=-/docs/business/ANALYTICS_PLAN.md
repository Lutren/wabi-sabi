# ANALYTICS_PLAN

Status: draft.

## Principles

- Collect the minimum needed.
- Avoid invasive tracking.
- Keep analytics out of open-source packages unless optional.
- Disclose analytics in privacy docs.

## Website

- Page views.
- CTA clicks.
- Download clicks.
- Gumroad/Buy Me a Coffee outbound clicks.

## Apps

Default: no telemetry unless explicitly enabled.

Potential opt-in:

- version;
- platform;
- crash class;
- feature usage counts.

No user content or secrets.
