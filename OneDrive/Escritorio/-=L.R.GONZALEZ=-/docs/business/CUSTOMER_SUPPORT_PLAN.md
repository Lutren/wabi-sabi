# CUSTOMER_SUPPORT_PLAN

Status: draft gated. `LEGAL_REVIEW_REQUIRED`.

Operational decision 2026-05-01: use `medioevo.saga@gmail.com` as the pilot/MVP commercial support inbox because it is already present on public surfaces. A branded domain alias can replace it later, but it is not required for local QA or draft listings.

## Channels

- Website contact page.
- Gumroad customer messages.
- Buy Me a Coffee posts/messages.
- Pilot/MVP support inbox: `medioevo.saga@gmail.com`.
- Optional later alias: `soporte@medioevo.space` or another branded mailbox if domain mail is configured.

## Response Targets

- Critical purchase/download issue: 2 business days.
- App bug: 5 business days.
- Feature request: triage monthly.

## Required Per Product

- product name/version;
- platform;
- purchase source;
- issue description;
- logs/screenshots if safe;
- no secrets in support messages.

## Not Accepted In Support

- API keys, tokens, private keys or passwords.
- Private RPG/game assets or unpublished lore.
- Unnecessary personal, medical, financial or biometric data.
- Full customer databases unless a paid support agreement explicitly covers a sanitized sample.
