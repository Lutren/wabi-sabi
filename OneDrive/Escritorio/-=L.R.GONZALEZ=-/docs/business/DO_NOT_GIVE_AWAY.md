# DO_NOT_GIVE_AWAY

Do not include these in free/open releases:

- private game source;
- TCG source, card data, deck assets, game bridge;
- full books unless explicitly approved;
- paid companion PDFs;
- commercial app source unless explicitly approved;
- Gumroad product ZIPs;
- private lore;
- unpublished assets;
- secrets and sessions;
- vendors and pentest repos.
