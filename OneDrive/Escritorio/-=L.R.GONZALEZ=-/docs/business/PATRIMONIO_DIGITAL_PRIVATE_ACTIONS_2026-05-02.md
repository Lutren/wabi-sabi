   operationally, who the human successors are, and what still requires legal,

## Non-Negotiable

No token values, passwords, recovery codes, private keys, bank data or medical/legal documents go into this workspace document.

## Wabi-Sabi Local Stewardship Boundary

The local/private Wabi-Sabi system is allowed to act as an operational
continuity steward for family support, project preservation, documentation,
handoffs and digital patrimony. This is separate from the public/commercial
Wabi-Sabi product.

It can prepare, prioritize, document and coordinate. It cannot replace signed
legal authority, medical decisions, tax/accounting advice, beneficiary changes,
bank access, custody decisions or irreversible transfer of private IP. Those
must be handled through human successors and professional documents.
