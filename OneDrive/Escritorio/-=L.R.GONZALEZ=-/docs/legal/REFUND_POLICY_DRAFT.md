## Proposed Policy

Digital products may be refunded when:

- the download is broken and cannot be corrected;
- the product was purchased by mistake and not downloaded;
- the product description materially misrepresented contents.

Refunds may be declined when:

- the full digital product was downloaded and functions as described;
- the request asks for private source code or excluded content;
- the purchase was outside the supported platform.

Final policy requires legal review and platform-specific alignment.
