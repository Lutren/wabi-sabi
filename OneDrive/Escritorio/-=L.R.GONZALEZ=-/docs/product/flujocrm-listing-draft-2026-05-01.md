## Product Name

FlujoCRM

## Short Description

A local-first desktop CRM for small businesses that want contacts, follow-ups
and a simple sales pipeline without a monthly SaaS subscription.

## Long Description

FlujoCRM helps small businesses, freelancers and operators keep customer work in
one local desktop app: contacts, pipeline stages, follow-up tasks and activity
notes.

It is designed for people who need something more focused than a spreadsheet
but do not want to move every customer workflow into a cloud CRM.

Your working database is local on your computer. There is no hosted account,
login portal or recurring SaaS subscription in the base app.

## Who It Is For

- Small service businesses.
- Freelancers and consultants.
- Local agencies and operators.
- People tracking leads, clients, follow-ups and simple opportunities.

## Included At First Release

- Windows x64 installer.
- Local contacts and company fields.
- Simple pipeline workflow.
- Follow-up tasks.
- Local app storage.

- Email support through `medioevo.saga@gmail.com` during the pilot/MVP period.

support and install documentation can stay narrow.

## Not Included

- Mobile app.
- Multi-user team accounts.
- Guaranteed sales results.
- Source code access by default.
