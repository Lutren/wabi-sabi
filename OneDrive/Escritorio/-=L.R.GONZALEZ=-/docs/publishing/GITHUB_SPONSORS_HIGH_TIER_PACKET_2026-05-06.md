## Do Not Offer

- Raw workspace research dumps.
- Private runtime prompts or orchestration internals.
- Secrets, credentials, account access or local sessions.
- Full unpublished books, private canon vaults or editorial source.
- RPG/TCG source, mechanics, bridge code, lore or assets.
- Real social datasets, private backtests or prediction claims.
- Medical, legal, financial, security or scientific guarantees.
- Unlimited consulting or support without a separate written agreement.

## Falsifiers

- Public page does not show all three new tiers after claimed publication.
- A tier says or implies raw research access.
- A tier exposes private runtime, unpublished books, RPG/TCG, secrets or datasets.
- A tier promises guaranteed safety, prediction, diagnosis or validated science.
- Dashboard edit is claimed without post-action evidence.
