# Support

Basic purchase support covers:

- access to the ZIP;
- clarification of what each template is for;
- help identifying which claims should remain blocked.

Support does not include:

- publishing your study as validated science;
- domain-specific medical, legal, financial or safety advice;
- access to DUAT Geodia, MEDIOEVO RPG/TCG, private Claudio runtime or private
  datasets.

For implementation support, use a separate paid support agreement.
