# Patent Pattern Map

Status: `RESEARCH_ONLY` and `LEGAL_REVIEW_REQUIRED`.

This map translates patent-adjacent material into abstract engineering patterns.
It is not patent clearance, legal advice, biomedical guidance, therapeutic
authorization, scientific validation of Observacionismo, or product marketing.

## Rule

Use only the abstract software pattern:

```text
global signal -> authorized receptor -> local threshold/gate -> evidence and residue ledger -> reversible action or human review
```

Do not copy patent claims, wet-lab methods, device instructions, genetic
engineering workflows, medical parameters or therapeutic language into products.
